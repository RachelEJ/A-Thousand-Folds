Square Box

The Square Box is a traditional model that can actually function to store object inside. For safe keeping, however, it is recommended to make a second box as a lid.

  • Step 1

    The paper here is 15cm x 15cm, single-sided origami paper. Any paper cut into the shape of a square should be usable.

  • Step 2

    Fold the left edge over to meet the right edge. Crease then unfold.

  • Step 3

    Fold the top edge down to meet the bottom edge and crease.

  • Step 4

    Unfold.

  • Step 5

    Turn the paper over.

  • Step 6

    Fold the top right corner down to the bottom left corner. Crease then unfold.

  • Step 7

    Fold the top left corner down to the bottom right corner and crease.

  • Step 8

    Unfold.

  • Step 9

    Fold the top right and bottom left corners in to the center point.

  • Step 10

    Repeat with the top left and bottom right corners.

  • Step 11

    Fold the bottom right edge to meet the right diagonal center line.

  • Step 12

    fold the top left edge to meet the right diagonal center line.

  • Step 13

    Unfold Steps 11 and 12.

  • Step 14

    Unfold Step 10.

  • Step 15

    Fold the bottom left edge in to meet the left diagonal center line.

  • Step 16

    Fold the top right edge in to meet the left diagonal center line.

  • Step 17

    Unfold Steps 15 and 16.

  • Step 18

    Fold along the creases in Steps 15 and 16 to make them stand at 90°.

  • Step 19

    Fold the top left side up while using the previously made creases as guides.

  • Step 20

    Fold the top left side down over the other two sides of the box to make the top left wall.

  • Step 21

    Turn the model 180°.

  • Step 22

    Repeat Step 19.

  • Complete

    Repeat Step 20. You have completed the Square Box.
