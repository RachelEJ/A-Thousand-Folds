Panda (Page 1)

Designed by Jacky Chan (not martial artist Jackie Chan), the Panda is a more advanced model with many small folds and the occasional fold without a reference point. It is not recommended for beginners. Click on a picture to enlarge it if necessary.

  • Step 1

    The paper here is 15cm x 15cm, single-sided origami paper. Any paper cut into the shape of a square should be usable.

  • Step 2

    With the paper at a diagonal, fold the bottom corner up to the top corner. Crease then unfold.

  • Step 3

    Turn the paper over then orient it so that the previously folded crease is vertical.

  • Step 4

    Fold the bottom corner up to the top corner. Crease then unfold.

  • Step 5

    Turn the paper over so that the colored side is up.

  • Step 6

    Fold the bottom corner up to the center and crease.

  • Step 7

    Fold the top corner down to the center and crease.

  • Step 8

    Unfold Steps 6 and 7.

  • Step 9

    Fold the bottom corner up to the point in which the top horizontal line and the vertical center line intersect. Crease.

  • Step 10

    Unfold.

  • Step 11

    Fold the top corner down to the point in which the last horizontal line and the vertical center line intersect. Crease then unfold.

  • Step 12

    Turn the paper over.

  • Step 13

    Fold the bottom corner up to the point in which the second horizontal line and the vertical center line intersect. Crease then unfold.

  • Step 14

    Fold the bottom corner up to the point in which the fourth horizontal line and the vertical center line intersect. Crease then unfold.

  • Step 15

    Fold the top corner down to the point in which the vertical center line intersects the line below the horizontal center line. Crease then unfold.

  • Step 16

    Fold the top corner down to the point in which the vertical center line intersects the line above the horizontal center line. Crease then unfold.

  • Step 17

    Turn the paper like so. There should be nine lines running diagonally one way and 1 line running diagonally the other way.

  • Step 18

    Fold the bottom edge up to meet the top edge and crease.

  • Step 19

    Fold the top edge down to meet the bottom edge and crease.

  • Step 20

    Fold the bottom edge up to meet the top edge of the previously folded rectangle.

  • Step 21

    Unfold Steps 18 through 20.

  • Step 22

    Turn the paper 90째 counter-clockwise.

  • Step 23

    Repeat Steps 18 through 20.

  • Step 24

    Unfold everything and crease orient the paper so that the corner with the most creases points towards you.

  • Step 25

    Fold the bottom corner up to meet the lowest set of intersecting lines.

  • Step 26

    Turn the model over.

  • Step 27

    Bring the right edge over to meet the vertical center line, but DO NOT crease all the way. On the white side, there will be several sets of lines running from the bottom left to the top right. Counting from the bottom, the second line is where you'll stop creasing. Unfold.

  • Step 28

    Repeat on the left side.

  • Step 29

    The paper should look like this so far.

  • Step 30

    Imagine a line running between the points where the recently folded lines and the outermost diagonal lines intersect.

  • Step 31

    Valley fold along that line. DO NOT crease all the way, but only on the line.

  • Step 32

    Take the right edge and valley fold it along the outermost right line. Flatten the part towards the bottom so that the left edge aligns with the vertical center line.

  • Step 33

    Repeat on the left side.

  • Step 34

    Take the set of folds just right of the vertical center line and start a mountain fold on the first and a valley fold on the second.

  • Step 35

    Pleat fold.

  • Step 36

    Repeat on the other side.

  • Step 37

    Turn the model 180째. Open the left flap.

  • Step 38

    Bring the flap's main corner to meet the intersecting diagonal lines in the middle of the model.

  • Step 39

    Flatten and crease everything.

  • Step 40

    Rotate 180째.

  • Step 41

    Repeat on the other side.

  • Step 42

    Mountain fold along the horizontal center line. It is located along the same axis in which the black corners point towards each other.

  • Step 43

    Turn the model over so that you can see the V made by the two corners.

  • Step 44

    Valley fold to make the triangles on the sides of the black center piece meet up with the black triangles on the back piece.

  • Step 45

    Rotate 180째. Open the previous fold slightly and crease fold the layer on the left side over onto the center part.

  • Step 46

    This will fold a diagonal underneath the black center piece. Flatten and crease.

  • Step 47

    Pull out the triangle under the black center piece.

  • Step 48

    Arrange it so that it makes an "S" when looking from the right side.

  • Step 49

    Flatten and crease. The edges should line up as pictured.

  • Step 50

    Fold the bottom left edge of the left flap up to meet the horizontal center line.

  • Alternate View

    Close-up of Step 50

  • Step 51

    Open the flap and the black center piece slightly.

  • Step 52

    Tuck part of the flap from Step 50 underneath the black center piece.

  • Step 53

    Flatten and crease the model.

  • Step 54

    The finished left side should look as pictured.

  • Step 55

    Repeat on the right side.
