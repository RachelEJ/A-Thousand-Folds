Waterbomb

Being the source of the Waterbomb Base's name, the Waterbomb is next to the Crane as one of the most iconic origami models to date. The traditional model is a classic among beginners and veterans alike due to its simplicity.

  • Step 1

    The paper here is 15cm x 15cm, single-sided origami paper. Any paper cut into the shape of a square should be usable.

  • Step 2

    Turn the paper over.

  • Step 3

    Fold the top down to meet the bottom edge. Crease then unfold.

  • Step 4

    Turn the paper 90Â°.

  • Step 5

    Fold the top edge down to the bottom and crease.

  • Step 6

    Lift the left flap while keeping the right side flat.

  • Step 7

    Open the flap.

  • Step 8

    Bring the creased edge to the center line.

  • Step 9

    Squash fold and crease.

  • Step 10

    Take the topmost right flap and fold it back over to the left.

  • Step 11

    Lift the right flap and bring the creased edge to the center line.

  • Step 12

    Squash fold and crease.

  • Step 13

    Take the topmost left flap and fold it back over to the right.

  • Step 14

    Fold the topmost left flap and bring its bottom edge to the center line. Crease.

  • Step 15

    Repeat with the topmost right flap.

  • Step 16

    Turn the model over.

  • Step 17

    Repeat Steps 14 and 15.

  • Step 18

    Take the top corner of the left flap and bring it to the bottom corner. Pinch the centermost part.

  • Step 19

    Repeat with the top right corner.

  • Step 20

    Unfold both flaps. You should see faint lines running horizontally through the middle.

  • Step 21

    Fold the topmost left corner to the center of the model. Crease.

  • Step 22

    Repeat with the topmost right corner.

  • Step 23

    Fold the top left corner down to the center of the model and crease.

  • Step 24

    Repeat with the top right corner.

  • Step 25

    Fold both flaps over the folded in left and right corners. Crease. It should resember the collar of a shirt.
