Angelfish

The Angelfish is a moderate model requiring no complex movements aside from outside reverse folds. It is a fun model to fold while also providing a challenge with its symmetrical nature.

  • Step 1

    The paper here is 15cm x 15cm, single-sided origami paper. It is currently folded into the Bird Base.

  • Step 2

    Fold the topmost right flap over to the left side.

  • Step 3

    Fold the bottom flap up so that the corners meet and crease.

  • Step 4

    Take the previously folded flap and fold it down to the right so that the left edge meets the horizontal center line. Crease then unfold.

  • Step 5

    Take the same flap and fold it down to the left so that the right edge meets the horizontal center line. Crease then unfold.

  • Step 6

    Rabbit ear fold.

  • Step 7

    Fold the rabbit ear fold flap to the right and crease.

  • Step 8

    Fold the flap up to the top left and crease.

  • Step 9

    Fold the the whole thing down and crease.

  • Step 10

    Turn the model over.

  • Step 11

    Repeat Step 3.

  • Step 12

    Repeat Step 4.

  • Step 13

    Repeat Step 5.

  • Step 14

    Repeat Steps 6 and 7.

  • Step 15

    Repeat Step 8.

  • Step 16

    Repeat Step 9.

  • Step 17

    Turn the model 90Â° clockwise.

  • Step 18

    Take the large flaps on the right side and inside reverse fold them so that the creased edge meets the vertical center line. Crease the edges.

  • Step 19

    Take the previously folded flap and fold the small corner inwards so that the bottom edge meets the edge of the square.

  • Step 20

    Fold all the small corners in.

  • Step 21

    Outside reverse fold the top fin.

  • Step 22

    Fold it so that the straight line from the square part is continuous.

  • Step 23

    Turn the model over and repeat.

  • Complete

    You have completed the Angelfish.
