Macaw

The Macaw is certainly not a model for a beginner. Designed by Manuel Sirgo, it features steps in which several layers of paper are folded over each other, leaving the paper prone to ripping. Extra caution is advised and good luck is wished to those who attempt this model.

  • Step 1

    The paper here is 15cm x 15cm, single-sided origami paper. It is currently folded into the Preliminary/Square Base with the open end pointing towards you.

  • Step 2

    Fold the bottom left and right edges to meet the vertical center line and crease.

  • Step 3

    Fold the top triangle down. Crease and unfold back to Step 2.

  • Step 4

    Petal fold and crease.

  • Step 5

    Fold the top part back down and crease.

  • Step 6

    Turn the model over.

  • Step 7

    Squash fold the left flap.

  • Step 8

    Fold the bottom left and right edges in to meet the vertical center line and crease.

  • Step 9

    Unfold.

  • Step 10

    Petal fold and crease.

  • Step 11

    Fold the top triangle down and crease.

  • Step 12

    Take the triangle and push the vertex to meet the right corner with the paper in between going up to form a point.

  • Step 13

    Repeat on the left side. The model should form a small M.

  • Step 14

    Take the right side and fold the top edge down to meet the bottom edge. Crease.

  • Step 15

    Repeat on the left side. The model should look as pictured.

  • Step 16

    Fold the whole left section back over to its side.

  • Step 17

    Squash fold the right flap.

  • Step 18

    Repeat Steps 8 through 15.

  • Step 19

    Fold the whole right section back over to its side.

  • Step 20

    Fold the topmost layer's top left edge in to meet the vertical center line and crease.

  • Step 21

    Repeat on the right side.

  • Step 22

    Fold the whole left section to the right.

  • Step 23

    Fold the top right edge in to meet the vertical center line.

  • Step 24

    Fold the whole left and right sections to the left.

  • Step 25

    Fold the top left edge in to meet the vertical center line.

  • Step 26

    Fold the whole right section back over to the right.

  • Step 27

    Turn the model over.

  • Step 28

    Fold the large flap up and crease.

  • Step 29

    Valley fold along the vertical center line. Orient the model as pictured.

  • Step 30

    Fold the bottom right edge in to meet the edge of the main body.

  • Step 31

    Fold the bottom right edge in to meet the edge of the main body again.

  • Step 32

    Turn the model over.

  • Step 33

    Repeat Steps 30 and 31.

  • Step 34

    Turn the model 90° counter-clockwise so that you are viewing the posterior side.

  • Step 35

    Fold the left wing down and crease.

  • Step 36

    Fold the right wing down and crease.

  • Step 37

    Fold the final flap down and crease.

  • Step 38

    Unfold.

  • Step 39

    Turn the model 180°.

  • Step 40

    Fold the flap back over a little and crease.

  • Step 41

    This should form the creases for a pleat fold.

  • Alternate View

    Step 41 from a side angle.

  • Step 42

    Fold along the vertical center line. Orient the model as pictured.

  • Step 43

    Pinch the corners in to round off the wings.

  • Step 44

    Repeat on the left side.

  • Step 45

    Fold the top over. Crease and unfold.

  • Step 46

    Inside reverse fold.

  • Step 47

    Go about halfway down and fold the tip down. Crease and unfold.

  • Step 48

    Outside reverse fold.

  • Step 49

    Inside reverse fold the tip of the beak.

  • Step 50

    Fold the ends of the feet down. Crease and unfold.

  • Step 51

    Inside reverse fold.

  • Step 52

    Repeat Steps 51 and 52.

  • Step 53

    Go about two thirds of the way down the smallest inner triangle.

  • Step 54

    Pinch the left side and flatten the right side.

  • Step 55

    Bend the tail at an angle.

  • Complete

    You have completed the Macaw.
