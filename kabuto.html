Kabuto

Fairly easy for beginners, the Kabuto is a traditional model of a samurai helmet. Using a larger square of paper may result in a wearable helmet.

  • Step 1

    The paper here is 15cm x 15cm, single-sided origami paper. Any paper cut into the shape of a square should be usable.

  • Step 2

    Turn the paper over and 45Â° to have one corner pointing towards you.

  • Step 3

    Fold the top corner down to meet the bottom corner. Crease.

  • Step 4

    Fold the left corner over to meet the right corner. Crease then unfold.

  • Step 5

    Fold the right corner down to the bottom corner. The top line should meet the vertical center line.

  • Step 6

    Repeat with the left side.

  • Step 7

    Fold both flaps up to meet the top corner.

  • Step 8

    Fold them out at an angle to make the crest of the helmet.

  • Step 9

    Fold the topmost bottom layer about halfway up the helmet. Make sure the vertical center lines stay aligned. Crease.

  • Step 10

    Fold the rest of the topmost bottom layer up over the bottom of the helmet.

  • Step 11

    Open the model's bottom pocket.

  • Step 12

    Tuck the final layer into the pocket and crease.

  • Complete

    Shape the model. You have completed the Kabuto.
