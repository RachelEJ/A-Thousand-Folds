Canoe

The canoe is a classic model that, while looking more complex, is fairly easy. Thicker paper is recommended for it to float, however.

  • Step 1

    The paper here is 15cm x 15cm, single-sided origami paper. Any paper cut into the shape of a square should be usable.

  • Step 2

    Turn the paper over.

  • Step 3

    Fold the top down to meet the bottom edge and crease.

  • Step 4

    Unfold.

  • Step 5

    Fold the bottom edge to meet the horizontal center line and crease.

  • Step 6

    Repeat on with the top edge.

  • Step 7

    Fold the four corners in to meet the horizontal center line. Crease.

  • Step 8

    Take the left side of the model and fold the diagonal edges in to meet the horizontal center line. Crease.

  • Step 9

    Repeat with the right side. The right side's creases will overlap with the left's.

  • Step 10

    Fold the top and bottom corners into the center

  • Complete

    Turn the model inside out. You have completed the Canoe.
