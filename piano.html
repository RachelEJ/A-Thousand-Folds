Piano

The piano is a very simple model making it a good starter while introducing someone to origami. It can be used to teach basic mountain and valley folds and the squash fold.

  • Step 1

    The paper here is 15cm x 15cm, single-sided origami paper. Any paper cut into the shape of a square should be usable.

  • Step 2

    Turn the paper over.

  • Step 3

    Fold the top down to meet the bottom edge and crease.

  • Step 4

    Fold the right edge over to meet the left edge. Crease then unfold.

  • Step 5

    Fold the left edge to meet the vertical center line and crease.

  • Step 6

    Repeat with the right side.

  • Step 7

    Open the left flap.

  • Step 8

    Squash fold and crease.

  • Step 9

    Open the right flap.

  • Step 10

    Squash fold and crease.

  • Step 11

    Fold the bottom center flap up to meet the top edge and crease.

  • Step 12

    Fold the left and right sides back into the center and crease.

  • Complete

    Open the folds made in Steps 11 and 12. Shape the model. You have completed the Piano.
