Elephant

Designed by Fumiaki Kawahata, this elephant includes many complex folds that, if not performed correctly, may affect the alignment of the whole model. Successfully folded, however, it is very satisfying and pleasing to the eye.

  • Step 1

    The paper here is 15cm x 15cm, single-sided origami paper. Any paper cut into the shape of a square should be usable.

  • Step 2

    Fold the top right corner down to the bottom left corner. Crease then unfold.

  • Step 3

    Fold the top left corner down to the bottom right corner and crease.

  • Step 4

    Unfold.

  • Step 5

    Fold the top right corner down to the center point and crease.

  • Step 6

    Bring the top left corner over so that the top edge meets the diagonal edge. DO NOT crease all the way. Only pinch on the corner's diagonal crease.

  • Step 7

    Unfold. The paper should look as pictured.

  • Step 8

    Fold the bottom right corner up to the point made in Step 6. Crease and unfold.

  • Step 9

    Fold the bottom right corner up to the center point and crease.

  • Step 10

    Fold the other two corners in to the center point and crease.

  • Step 11

    Unfold.

  • Step 12

    Take the bottom right corner and bring it to the line to the bottom right of the diagonal center line. DO NOT crease all the way. Only pinch the corner's diagonal crease.

  • Step 13

    Take the top left corner and bring it to the previously made pinch. DO NOT crease all the way. Only pinch the corner's diagonal crease.

  • Step 14

    Fold the top left corner to the previously made pinch and crease.

  • Step 15

    Unfold.

  • Step 16

    Fold the top right corner down to the furthest line in the bottom left corner. Crease then unfold.

  • Step 17

    Fold the top left corner up to the furthest line in the top right corner and crease.

  • Step 18

    Unfold.

  • Step 19

    Rotate the paper 45Â° clockwise.

  • Step 20

    Take the pair of lines right under the horizontal center line. Valley fold on the first one and crease mountain fold on the second.

  • Step 21

    Take the pair of lines right before the top corner. Valley fold on the first and crease mountain fold on the second.

  • Step 22

    Fold the remainder of the top corner down and crease.

  • Step 23

    Fold the left edge up to meet the imaginary line running between the two vertical parallel lines. Crease then unfold.

  • Alternate View

    Close up of Step 23.

  • Step 24

    Repeat Step 23 on the left side. Crease then unfold.

  • Step 25

    Begin a rabbit ear fold.

  • Step 26

    Fold the flap to the right. Crease and unfold.

  • Step 27

    Fold the flap to the left. Crease and unfold.

  • Step 28

    Mountain fold on the vertical center line. Leave the previously folded flap to stand straight up.

  • Step 29

    Open the right side.

  • Step 30

    Squash fold.

  • Step 31

    Fold the left flap back over.

  • Step 32

    Lift the topmost layer.

  • Step 33

    Use the previously folded creases as guides to collapse the flap. The flap as a whole will be valley folded just under the top spine. The crease previously to the left of the vertical center line will be mountain folded.

  • Step 34

    Flatten the flap and crease. The corner should line up with the top spine.

  • Alternate View

    Step 35 as seen from the right side down the top spine.

  • Step 35

    Turn the model over.

  • Step 36

    Repeat Steps 33 and 34.

  • Step 37

    Fold the topmost flap down.

  • Step 38

    Turn the model over.

  • Step 39

    Fold the topmost flap down.

  • Step 40

    Fold the left triangle over the rest of the model. Crease then unfold.

  • Step 41

    Squash fold.

  • Step 42

    Fold the right flap back up.

  • Step 43

    Take the square towards the bottom of the model and lift it.

  • Step 44

    Fold it so that the bottom edge of the square meets the top edge of the model. Make sure to pull out the other layers of paper as you fold.

  • Step 45

    Squash fold the triangle on the left side at the same time and crease.

  • Step 46

    Open the square's topmost layer.

  • Step 47

    Bring the square's top left corner down to meet the bottom corner of the triangle, squash fold, and crease.

  • Step 48

    Turn the model over.

  • Step 49

    Repeat Steps 43 through 45.

  • Step 50

    Repeat Steps 46 and 47.

  • Step 51

    Take the topmost layer of the bottom left edge and fold it to the top right. Crease.

  • Step 52

    Fold part of the same flap to the left so that the crease on the triangle's edge meets the diagonal crease on the rest of the model.

  • Alternate View

    Close up of Step 52.

  • Step 53

    Open the layer starting at the right of the fold made in Step 52.

  • Step 54

    Flatten it so that the right square overlaps the left side.

  • Alternate View

    Close up of Step 54.

  • Step 55

    Turn the model over.

  • Step 56

    Repeat Steps 51 through 54.

  • Step 57

    Directly to the right of the piece folded in Step 56, take the topmost flap and fold it down so that the top right edge meets the horizontal center line. Crease then unfold.

  • Step 58

    Take the same flap and fold it inside using the same crease.

  • Step 59

    Turn the model over.

  • Step 60

    Repeat Steps 57 and 58

  • Step 61

    Fold the bottom right corner up to continue the line made by the part to the left.

  • Step 62

    Inside reverse fold.

  • Step 63

    Turn the model over.

  • Step 64

    Repeat Steps 61 and 62.

  • Step 65

    Take the small triangle in the bottom right and valley fold on its top left edge. Crease then unfold.

  • Step 66

    Inside reverse fold.

  • Step 67

    Turn the model over.

  • Step 68

    Repeat Steps 65 and 66.

  • Step 69

    Take the topmost left flap and fold it over.

  • Step 70

    Fold the left edge in to meet the horizontal center line.

  • Step 71

    Repeat on the right side.

  • Step 72

    Fold the flap back over.

  • Step 73

    Fold the trunk up to continue the line made by the ears. Crease then unfold.

  • Step 74

    Inside reverse fold.

  • Step 75

    Inside reverse fold the tip of the trunk.

  • Step 76

    Take the corner on the head and fold it in.

  • Step 77

    Repeat on the other side.

  • Step 78

    Curl the ears

  • Step 79

    Open the bottom to make a triangle-like shape.

  • Step 80

    Turn to see the back.

  • Step 81

    Fold the small white rectangle back up into the bottom. This will make the model stand on its own.

  • Step 82

    Looking at the front part, round off the paper to make the front legs.

  • Complete

    You have completed the Elephant.
